New Discoveries Today

During my html adventure i discovered that they are many html elements that you can guess what they are used for from the name of the elements or the abbrevitions.
For example we have; the

  1. Button
    <button><\button>
  2. Paragraph 
    <p><\p>
  3. Image
    <img>
  4. Audio 
    <audio><\audio>
And they are those that you can't easily depict their meaning from their tag name.
For example; 
  1. <hr>
  2. <base>
  3. <meta>
  4. <wbr>
